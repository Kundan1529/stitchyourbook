Who we Are

At Gayathri Machinery, we craft the invisibe yet indispensable-stitching
wires with uncompromising attention to detail.

Started in 1964, our company has a rich experience in the printing machinery and ancillary industries, now holding the largest installed manufacturing capacity of stitching wires in India.

With decades of experiecne, we specialize in stitching wires that seamlessly hold your books, brochures, and magazines together.

Our Journey So Far

The print industry once overlooked stitching wires, seeing them as minor components. We changed that narrative by making quality a priority.
Today, we hold a 45% market share in India and export to more than 30 countries, with our wires being the preferred choice for leading manufactures-being used in 72 out of 100 notebooks in India.

Why We Do What We Do

Mission

Our mission is to provide the highest-quality consumables and machines that drive excellence in printing and minimize waste while promoting sustainability.

By prioritizing innovation, we aim to deliver efficient, superior printing solutions that make quality printing accessible to all.

Vision

To be the most trusted and preferred partner in the global printing and binding industry, setting benchmarks for quality, reliability, and customer satisfaction through advanced technology and ethical practices.
